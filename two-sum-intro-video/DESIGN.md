# Two Sum Intro Video Design

## Style Prompt

Clean, restrained product-showcase intro for AI Interview Coach Agent. The piece should feel like the opening beat of a real engineering demo: trustworthy, calm, and rooted in the actual product UI. Use blurred real-page captures as the visual field, with layered white and pale-blue surfaces for typography. Motion should guide attention, not sell hype.

## Colors

- Background: `#faf8ff`
- Surface: `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.86)`
- Surface raised: `rgba(234, 237, 255, 0.92)`
- Primary: `#005a90`
- Primary bright: `#0073b7`
- Text: `#131b2e`
- Muted text: `#404750`
- Accent line: `rgba(0, 90, 144, 0.18)`
- Success: `#15803d`

## Typography

- Headlines and body: `Microsoft YaHei`, sans-serif
- Labels and technical metadata: `JetBrains Mono`, monospace

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic dark gradients or neon tech visuals.
- Do not introduce a portrait or talking-head layer.
- Do not make the intro look like a marketing landing page.
- Do not detach the intro from the real ai-study page language.
